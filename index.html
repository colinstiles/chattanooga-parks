Welcome to the Chattanooga Parks & Trails Explorer

This interactive map displays public parks and trails throughout the greater Chattanooga area, with detailed information on addresses, amenities, trail types and lengths, and accessibility features. Our team at TnT Maps built this tool to encourage you, our neighbors and friends, to get outside and take advantage of some of Chattanooga's outdoor spaces.

How to use: In the upper-right corner, you can toggle between different base maps and turn on/off the map's layers (parks and trails). The upper-left corner has an address search bar. The lower-right corner has map zoom controls, or you can use your touchscreen, mouse, or touchpad zoom controls. Click on a park or trail in the map to view the pop-up information for each feature.

Data Source: Data is sourced from the TREC Project (Trails-Recreation-Environment-Community) and edited by the author.

Disclaimer: This web map is for informational purposes only and is not intended to be used as an authoritative source for navigation or planning. The data in this map is not guaranteed to be 100% accurate in its spatial or attribute information.

Repository: View the source code and access the data on GitHub. If you find an error or missing data, I encourage you to reach out to me via the GitHub Issues page in the provided link, or email me at TinAndTopo@gmail.com.
